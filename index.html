About Lavaflake.com

Lavaflake.com is the original site where LAS, or Last Artist Standing began under the administration of Lava.

  • Lava is anticipated to have passed away during their administration.
  • LAS transitioned to a period of unrest, but programmer w created a stand-in site to continue it until it was also taken down.
  • Today DAD.gallery stands as a testament to the concept of drawing every day, under the administration of the1banana.

Projects in the Work

I am currently working on multiple personal projects involving LAS/DAD currently. Updates can be found here.

  • DAD/LAS Wiki - Over time part of this site will transition from a project tracker to a wiki documenting the History of LAS from years back to present. Will try to recover Lavaflake Blog posts, Find original imagery, create a mockup of what the old pages were like. Add information on notable figures in the history of LAS and DAD.
  • The DAD.gallery Companion App - A mobile App for iOS that will give you reminders of the daily posting deadline, have a Daily Topic(Hell) for drawing.
  • Critr, a Community Art Critique App - Stay tuned.
